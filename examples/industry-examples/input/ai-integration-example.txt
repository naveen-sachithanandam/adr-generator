Product team wanted to add conversational AI assistant 
to customer self service portal. 
Customers ask questions about their accounts, 
policies, product features, and support issues.

Two options evaluated. 
Option one — connect directly to public LLM API. 
Option two — build RAG system using company's own knowledge base.

Direct LLM connection tested poorly. 
Model had no knowledge of company specific policies, 
pricing, or product details. Generated plausible but incorrect answers. 
Customer trust risk identified as high, as there would be hallucinations and trade-offs/proposals designed would be wrong.

Decided to implement RAG architecture. 
Company documentation, FAQs, policy documents, and product guides 
loaded into vector database. 
System retrieves relevant context before generating response.
Accuracy in testing improved significantly. 
Answers are grounded in actual company documentation.

Ongoing maintenance required to keep knowledge base current. 
Content team now owns update process. 
Hallucination rate dropped from 34 percent in testing 
to under 3 percent in production.
